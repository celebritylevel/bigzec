How AI Is Replacing $5K Creative Strategy Briefs in 20 Minutes

February 2026 · 4 min read

Agencies charge $3,000-$5,000 for a creative strategy brief. AI can produce one in 20 minutes. Here's the framework that makes it possible.

The Problem with Traditional Creative Strategy

Most creative strategies produce 20 ads that look different to humans but identical to Meta's Andromeda. The platform's visual recognition groups similar creatives into the same Entity ID. Your "fresh batch" of 15 ads registers as 2 or 3 concepts in the system.

That's why CPMs keep climbing even when you launch new creative every week. You're not actually providing new concepts to the algorithm.

The P.D.A. Framework

The solution is P.D.A.: Persona × Pain × Desire × Awareness. This framework ensures true creative diversity by systematically varying each dimension.

How P.D.A. Works

  • Persona: Who is the target? (VP Sales, Marketing Director, RevOps Lead)
  • Pain: What problem do they face? (Manual processes, poor visibility, slow ramp)
  • Desire: What outcome do they want? (Faster revenue, better forecasting, team efficiency)
  • Awareness: Where are they in the journey? (Unaware, problem-aware, solution-aware, product-aware)

Each unique combination creates a distinct creative concept that registers as genuinely different in ad platforms.

The AI Creative Strategy Workflow

Here's how AI produces a complete creative strategy in 20 minutes:

Step 1: Product URL Analysis

Paste a product URL. AI extracts positioning, features, and benefits. Confirm or adjust the extracted information.

Step 2: Persona Generation

AI generates 4-6 persona candidates based on the product. Select the ones that match your actual customers.

Step 3: Pain Point Mapping

AI maps pain points and desires for each persona. Approve, adjust, or redirect the analysis.

Step 4: P.D.A. Matrix Construction

AI builds the complete matrix of all combinations. You pick which concept cells to develop.

Step 5: Ad Generation

AI writes 5 ads per concept across all awareness stages. Approve each set before moving on.

Step 6: Andromeda Diversity Check

AI checks every ad against platform diversity requirements to ensure they register as distinct concepts.
