Scalable Influencer Research: How AI Agents Find Partners in Minutes

February 2026 · 5 min read

An agency needed 15 YouTube influencers for a client campaign. Using AI agents, they had a scalable system to find unlimited influencers in 30 minutes.

This isn't just for influencers—the same approach finds podcast guests, partnerships, affiliates, and leads in any niche.

The Traditional Influencer Research Problem

Finding influencers manually means hours of searching, channel-by-channel evaluation, and contact extraction. When you need 15 influencers, you might manually review 100+ channels. When you need to scale, the process breaks.

How AI Agents Automate Influencer Research

The workflow combines Claude Code with SERP APIs to create a repeatable system:

Step 1: SERP Scraping by Keywords

Use Bright Data's YouTube SERP API to scrape search results by relevant keywords. This pulls all channels matching your criteria in seconds.

Step 2: Channel Data Extraction

AI extracts relevant channel data: subscriber count, video views, upload frequency, content themes. It identifies which channels fit your ICP criteria.

Step 3: Enrichment

Run quick enrichment to add additional data points: engagement rates, audience demographics, recent sponsorships. This scores influencer fit.

Step 4: Contact Extraction

Use regex to extract emails from channel pages and descriptions. AI can also cross-reference with LinkedIn and other platforms for additional contact methods.

Step 5: Qualification and Scoring

AI scores each influencer based on your criteria: audience size, engagement rate, content relevance, brand safety. Output is a prioritized list ready for outreach.
